iotix specialises in cyber security for operational technology environments where availability and safety are paramount. With over 7 years of experience spanning both IT and OT domains, the consultancy brings focused expertise to industrial control systems, SCADA networks, IoT devices, and critical infrastructure.

The consultancy's core strength lies in developing robust security architectures that effectively bridge traditional IT systems with industrial operational technology. This includes security assessments, technology evaluation, implementation services, and architectural design that respects operational constraints.

Whether you're looking to evaluate security solutions, implement protective measures, or develop a comprehensive OT security programme, iotix delivers practical expertise that enhances your security posture without compromising critical operations.
